About Us

Established in the year 2016, "TECON TECHNOLOGIES LLP" is engaged in the process of importing, exporting and trading a technically advanced range of Electronic and Electrical Equipment. We are authorized distributor for EAO. Collaborating with companies having reputed products that are known for their high functionality and durability makes our product best in class. Our partners have manufacturing facilities located throughout Europe, namely Switzerland, Germany, France and Sweden and adhere to the strictest of quality norms. These product ranges are approved by al major certifying agencies and meet stringent quality norms. Tecon Technologies LLP Limited is catering to your prestigious requirement of various industrial and HMI products. Tecon Technologies has joined hands with EAO, Switzerland, Sweden for the supply of the products in India as well as the Middle East through its subsidiary Kolsons Middle East FZC to provide trusted and approved products for a variety of applications. Our aim is to render our best services to you in a timely and efficient manner and fulfill all your requirements

Authorized Dealer in Middle East


Human Machine Interfaces. Since 1947.
EAO AG, a Swiss, family-owned company founded in 1947, has developed into one of the world’s leading manufacturers of high-quality industrial switches, complete custom HMI panels and interface systems, and specialised automotive switch systems.

With over 600 dedicated employees, EAO has a global production and distribution network at its disposal. EAO has production sites in Switzerland, Germany, North America and China, as well as 10 wholly owned international sales companies and distributors in over 50 countries, so we can guarantee global availability – around the world, around the clock.
