About Kolsons Middle East

Established in the year 2016, "Kolsons Middle East" is engaged in the process of importing, exporting and trading a technically advanced range of Electronic and Electrical Equipment. We are authorized distributor for EAO. Collaborating with companies having reputed products that are known for their high functionality and durability makes our product best in class. Our partners have manufacturing facilities located throughout Europe, namely Switzerland, Germany, France and Sweden and adhere to the strictest of quality norms. These product ranges are approved by al major certifying agencies and meet stringent quality norms. Kolsons Middle East Limited is catering to your prestigious requirement of various industrial and HMI products. Tecon Technologies has joined hands with EAO, Switzerland, Sweden for the supply of the products in India as well as the Middle East through its subsidiary Kolsons Middle East FZC to provide trusted and approved products for a variety of applications. Our aim is to render our best services to you in a timely and efficient manner and fulfill all your requirements.
